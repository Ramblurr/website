IndieHosters

Want to help redecentralize the net, but you don't have the time and/or the skills to run your own server in your spare time? Then let Pierre or Michiel act as your personal hoster, helping you to have your own internet server and website, using free software and implementing open protocols, independent from the big monopolies.

This new project was previously called "Third Party People" in Unhosted Adventures. We're currently in closed alpha, and aim to go live in autumn 2014. More news here soon!
